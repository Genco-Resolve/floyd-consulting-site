Professional Background

I am a Registered Professional Accountant SA (SAIPA) and CIMA Strategy Level candidate, with over seven years of progressive experience across full-cycle accounting, outsourced finance, advisory support, and client-facing finance roles.

My career has been defined by the integration of technical finance execution with operational and stakeholder engagement. I believe that effective financial communication is built on accurate data, disciplined processes, and reliable reporting—principles that have shaped how I engage with business owners, operational teams, and clients.

Currently relocating to Zimbabwe, I am focused on serving established corporates across Zimbabwe and Southern Africa with finance leadership, transformation, and strategic advisory services.
